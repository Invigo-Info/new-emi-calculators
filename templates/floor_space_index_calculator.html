Understanding Floor Space Index (FSI)

What is FSI?

Floor Space Index (FSI) or Floor Area Ratio (FAR) is the ratio of total floor area to the plot area. It determines how much construction is allowed on a plot.

Formula

FSI = Total Floor Area รท Plot Area

Example

Plot Area: 1000 sq ft, Total Floor Area: 1500 sq ft
FSI = 1500 รท 1000 = 1.5

FSI Guidelines
0 - 0.5 Low Density - Bungalows/Villas

2.0 - 3.0 Very High Density - High-rise Buildings
3.0+ Ultra High Density - Skyscrapers
